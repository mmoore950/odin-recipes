Airfryer Boneless Chicken Thighs

Description

This chicken thigh recipe is a quick and delicious meal that anyone can make in only 20 minutes! By uising boneless and skinless thighs, not only does the chicken cook more evenly in the airfryer, but it is healthier, and involves less cleanup and grease than normal bone-in, skin-on thighs would.

If you are into clean, convienient, and delicious recipes, this is for you!

Ingredients

  • 1-1.5lbs of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Garlic Powder
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cayenne Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Olive Oil
  1. For this recipe, you are going to need 1-1.5lbs of boneless chicken thighs, or as many as can comfortably fit in your airfryer. Begin by coating them in olive oil, and your seasonings of choice.
  2. Once the chicken thighs are thoroughly seasoned, you should cook them in the airfryer for 12 minutes at 400 degrees F, flipping halfway through.
  3. Next, place the chicken thighs straight on a medium-high heat non stick pan that is already heated, such that the thighs can develop the crust that we are looking for. About 2 minutes each side.
  4. Finally, remove the chicken thighs from the pan, and let them rest for 5 minutes before eating. Enjoy!
